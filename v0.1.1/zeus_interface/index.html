Bins in detector space

The transfer matrix projects into a list of 153 bins with irregular x and Q2 spacing. The bin edges are provided in data/zeus_bin_edges.jl but can also be quickly and easily accessed with the helper function get_bin_info as shown below.

get_bin_info(10)
([800, 950], [0.059, 0.076])
